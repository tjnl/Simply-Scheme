### 6.3

Rewrite the folowing procedure using `cond` instead of the `if`s:

~~~ scheme
(define (sign number)
  (if (< number 0)
      'negative
      (if (= number 0)
          'zero
          'positive)))
~~~

***

~~~ scheme
(define (sign number)
  (cond ((< number 0) 'negative)
        ((= number 0) 'zero)
        (else 'positive)))
~~~
