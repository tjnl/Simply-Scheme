Draw a plumbing diagram for each of the following expressions:

~~~ scheme
(+ 3 4 5 6 7)

;;   ┌\3/──\4/──\5/──\6/──\7/┐

;;              25
~~~

~~~ scheme
;;     ┌\7/──────────\18/┐

;;     └───────┤ ├───────┘
~~~
