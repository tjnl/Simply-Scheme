### 3.1
Translate the arithmetic expressions \\((3 + 4) \times 5\\) and \\(3 + (4 \times 5)\\) into scheme expressions and into plumbing diagrams.

***

~~~ scheme
;; first expression

(* (+ 3 4) 5)
 
;;   ┌\3/─\4/┐
;;   │   +   │
;;   └──┤ ├──┘
;;     ┌\7/─\5/┐
;;     │   *   │
;;     └──┤ ├──┘
;;        35
~~~
~~~ scheme
;; second expression

(+ 3 (* 4 5))

;;   ┌\4/─\5/┐
;;   │   *   │
;;   └──┤ ├──┘
;;     ┌\20/─\3/┐
;;     │    +   │
;;     └───┤ ├──┘
;;         23
~~~
